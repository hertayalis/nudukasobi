Travis Kelce's 'compact but elegant' $1 million car collection from $400k Rolls-Royce Ghost to custo

Publish date: 2024-07-21

STAR Kansas City Chief's tight end Travis Kelce has been one of his generation's most prolific football players - his multi-million-dollar garage is evidence of his success.

Kelce's, 34, continued All-Star level play has spawned debates around his rank of all-time tight ends in NFL history, but a YouTube video said he filled his garage with some of the most sought-after vehicles.

The tight end's car collection includes a custom-made classic car and several high-end luxury vehicles, according to the Real Truck blog.

A YouTube video from a self-described car addict (@TheStrangeStories0208) called the collection of vehicles "compact but elegant."

The All-Pro player boasts some modest staples in his garage.

A 2014 GMC Terrain is his daily driver, the blog claimed

The SUV can be sold on the second-hand vehicle market for $3,000 to $15,000, depending on the state of the vehicle, according to Edmunds.

He also gifted a custom-built tailgating GMC Sierra pickup for a local firefighter and veteran, per local Kansas City publication The Pitch.

Kelce's affinity for mass-market trucks extends to the Ford F-150.

The player accepted a 2013 F-150 from his older brother and now-retired Philadelphia Eagle, Jason Kelce.

Travis accidentally left a spare key in the truck's glove compartment the day of the vehicle transfer - it was stolen 10 hours after he received the pickup, according to The New York Post.

Here are some of Kelce's more iconic vehicles in his garage:

CUSTOM CHEVELLE

Kelce purchased a 1970 Chevrolet Chevelle convertible, a sought after American classic muscle car.

He custom built several elements of the vehicle, including the burgundy brown paint and electric battery.

The car is now powered by the same battery as a Tesla Model S.

ASTON MARTIN VANQUISH

The sleek supercar was one of Kelce's first car purchases during his All-Pro professional career.

Kelce purchased the speedster for $300,000 in 2014, according to the blog.

The car infrequently makes it out of the garage, appearing during a few celebrity events and black-tie outings.

MERCEDES AMG G-63

The boxy Mercedes-Benz SUV has become a classic car for today's celebrity.

Psychologist Jo Hemmings exclusively told The U.S. Sun the SUV's purchase would give the tight end a "dopamine and reward hit."

"His cars are a much more predictable and stable reminder of how good that makes him feel," Hemmings said.

ROLLS-ROYCE GHOST

Kelce and girlfriend Taylor Swift, 33, were captured in the Ghost leaving a Chief's loss to the Buffalo Bills in mid-December.

The black Ghost is a hand-made, made-to-order luxury vehicle with dozens of custom specifications.
