Donna Matturri (Jeopardy) Bio, Age, Husband, Net Worth

Publish date: 2024-06-27

Donna Matturri is the new contestant on the stage of Jeopardy who is on the way to playing for the second game after two winning streaks. She came on the stage on 23rd June 2023, dethroning the five-day champion, Ben Goldstein.

Early Life

Donna Matturri was born on 30th September 1983 and she is living in Pickerington, Ohio. Donna received the degree of BA in English from Montclair University, a master’s in MLIS from Rutgers University (2012), and Digital Asset Management Certificate from Rutgers University School of Communication and Information (January 2023 to June 2023). She has also received a license and certification from Ohio Library Council.

Donna Matturri Career

Donna Matturri began her professional life in 2008 as the team lead customer service employee. She worked with ERS Hire as the team lead for nearly four years (until 2012). With the same skill category, i.e., customer service, she had another job as the reference librarian at Montclair Public Library. After that, she spent five years and one month at Pickerington Public Library (2013 to 2018). From May 2018 to June 2019, she was promoted to the designation of assistant director at Pickerington Public Library.

From June 2019 to March 2021, she worked as the technology and media librarian for Upper Arlington Public. She rejoined the company as the librarian in May 2021, and before that, she had a short period of three months with VINTAGE Aging working as the digital navigator.

Jeopardy Journey

After winning a game on 23rd June 2023, Donna returned to maintain her name and position. She was not doing well in the show, but in the second round of the second game, she picked up quite well and scored the highest. The other two contestants, Ryan, came in second place, and Caroline, came on this place. After the second round, the scores were like-

  • Donna – $18,800
  • Ryan – $11,600
  • Caroline – $7,400

After the final round, Caroline did not give the correct answer while the other two had the correct one, and the scoreboard became like –

  • Donna – $22,800
  • Ryan – $21,822
  • Caroline – $0

If Donna Matturri wins today’s game, along with the other next three games, she will probably appear in the Tournament of Champions. She will appear alongside other contestants named – Ray Lalonde, Ben Chan, and Cris Pannullo.
