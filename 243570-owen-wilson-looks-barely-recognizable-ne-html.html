Owen Wilson Looks Barely Recognizable in New Trailer for 'Loki' Check Out His Gray Hair

Publish date: 2024-07-12

Owen Wilson shows off a new grey-haired look in the new trailer for "Loki", which had fans amazed by the sliver hairdo as the star was completely unrecognizable.

The usually blond, Owen Wilson will be grey-haired and mustached in the new "Loki" series which made him completely unrecognizable in a new trailer released for the film.

Disney + recently released over 50 different trailers for new series and films for the streaming service. The streaming platform has 86.8 million subscribers and expects to have around 260 million subscribers by 2024.

Owen Wilson pictured at the Premiere for "She's Funny That Way" at Harmony Gold on August 19, 2015

Wilson will feature in the new "Loki", which is a part of the Marvel Universe. His character is not completely known, however from the trailer it looks like he will play some kind of officer.

Fans took to Twitter to share their surprise at Wilson's new look for the series. He was compared to John Slattery and Michael Douglas by two Twitter users, one wrote,

"I was NOT ready for Owen Wilson to age into 1992 Michael Douglas but I am here for it."

Tom Hiddleston will reprise his role as Loki in the Disney+ series and from the trailer, it looks like the main storyline with center around Loki and his story.

Fans are not only excited that Loki will get his own series but that Wilson will make an appearance in the show. There is no doubt that Wilson will add fantastic comedic energy to the show.

Disney+ shared this December all the new tv-series and movies it will be releasing next year, including "Loki" which will feature the grey-haired Wilson.

Twitter was buzzing with fans' excitement for all the new releases including new Star Wars series and reboots for different films. The streaming service captioned a video of footage of the new content read with,

"New adventures. New Originals. Endless possibilities. All of these and more are coming exclusively to #DisneyPlus."

Owen Wilson is grey-haired for new Loki Series. Photo: YouTube/Entertainment Tonight

Owen Wilson is known for his blond hair and catchphrase “wow” and has played numerous roles in his career including acting in the "Night of the Museum" movies and "Wedding Crashers."

However, he first wanted to be a screenwriter and his first-ever screenplay was "Bottle Rocket". Wes Anderson co-wrote and directed the movie. His brother Luke starred in the movie.

Ben Stiller and Wilson have always been an iconic duo, the two stars have actually been in 10 movies together, including "Zoolander" and the "Meet the Parents" movies.

Wilson is a critically acclaimed actor and screenwriter and has won many years throughout his career. The most recent award he received was in 2015, where he won the Robert Altman Award at the Independent Spirit Awards.
