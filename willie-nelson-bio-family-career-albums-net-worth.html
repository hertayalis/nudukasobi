Publish date: 2024-08-30

Willie Nelson Bio, Family, Career, Albums, Net Worth: Willie Nelson, officially known as Willie Hugh Nelson an American country musician, instrumentalist, and performing artiste.

His albums; Shotgun Willie, Red Headed Stranger, and Stardust made him one of the most recognized artists in country music. Willie Nelson was one of the leading figures of outlaw country, a subgenre of country music that developed in the late 1960s.

Willie Nelson is a major liberal activist and the co-chair of the advisory board of the National Organization for the Reform of Marijuana Laws (NORML), which is in favor of marijuana legalization.

Willie Nelson Parents

Willie Nelson was born to Ira Doyle Nelson (father) and Myrle Marie Nelson (mother). The couple tied the knot on Thursday, September 6, 1929, in Newton County, Arkansas.

Ira was born on July 09, 1913, in Searcy County, Arkansas, and died September 05, 1978, in Austin, Travis County. Myrle was born May 03, 1913, in Searcy County, Arkansas, and died December 1983 in Yakima, Washington.
