Sofia Vergara Glows in a Neon-Green Swimsuit on Italian Holiday

Publish date: 2024-07-28

Sofia Vergara is celebrating her birthday with a dreamy beach holiday.

The Modern Family actress has been in Italy with friends, celebrating ahead of her 51st birthday today. She has been documenting the trip on Instagram, sharing photos of her romantic dinner looks, day dresses, and swimwear.

Two idyllic snaps show her posing in front of a balcony with an ocean view, wearing nothing but a neon-green one-piece swimsuit. Her hair is down in relaxed waves and she wears bronzy makeup, including a maroon lip, and a white manicure. She holds onto the doors of the balcony as boats are seen sailing behind her.
