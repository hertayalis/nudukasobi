Elisabeth Moss's Changing Looks

Publish date: 2024-06-10

See Elisabeth Moss's looks through the years.

1997

An auburn-haired Moss attended the Beverly Hills premiere of A Thousand Acres.

1999

The Girl, Interrupted showed off a shag at the premiere.

2001

Blond curls for a charity gala.

2004

Now appearing on The West Wing, Moss wore her hair loose and natural for the Independent Spirit Awards.

2007

She pulled back her blond strands at the crown for the Paley Center tribute to Mad Men.

2008

A far cry from the sometimes dowdy Peggy Olson, Moss posed at the Paley Television Festival with flawless makeup and a voluminous retro-inspired 'do.

2008

For the Emmys, Moss styled her brunet locks into loose waves.

2010

Fresh fringe and a blown-out bob for the Golden Globes.

2010

At the International Emmy Awards, Moss complemented her highlighted mane with peach blush and matching gloss.

2011

The Golden Globes nominee wore her flaxen bob in face-flattering layers.

2011

Brunet again for the LACMA Inaugural Art + Film Gala. "I like that I get to go back and forth," Moss told InStyle. "I feel invigorated style-wise when I change the hair color. Although, I still think I'm blond sometimes and then I look in the mirror and I'm like, 'Oh.'"

2012

For a Golden Globes after-party, Moss styled her dark locks into a romantic updo.

2012

Wow! Moss went to Tommy Buckett and Caroline Buckler at the Marie Robinson Salon for her platinum pixie. "I tweaked the color a bit to go with this cut," Buckler said. "Elisabeth's skin tone is perfect for this shade, which makes her eyes really pop." Why the big change? "I had the same brunet color for two projects in a row, and I wanted to do something really different," Moss told us. "What's great about being blond is that you can wear any color-even darker colors. Blond hair is almost like an accessory in itself!"

2012

A brunette again! The On the Road star showed off a newly-dark crop at the Peace Over Violence Humanitarian Awards.

2013

The actress went back to blonde as Mad Men wrapped its sixth season. "What's great about being blonde is that you can wear any color, even darker colors," Moss told InStyle. "Blond hair is almost like an accessory in itself."

2014

The actress highlighted her blue eyes with bronze shadow and coppery hair hue for the The One I Love premiere in L.A.

2015

For the 2015 Tony Awards, Moss wore a bold pink lip and her platinum layers blown out smooth.

2016

Moss arrived at a Samsung event during the Sundance Film Festival with light blond locks and berry-colored lips.

2017

The Handmaid's Tale star went for a statement lip at a recent Top of the Lake photocall, opting for a bold orange-red hue.
