Ryujin (born 17th April 2001) is a South Korean dancer, rapper, and singer. The young popster is the main lead of the girl band ITZY.

Her group was formed by JYP Entertainment. Their other members are Yeji, Lia, Chaeryeong, and Yuna.

The 19-year-old is the main vocalist and rapper. Loved by fans all over the world, the young pop star is extremely popular amongst the teenage fan base. 

Let’s dive into more details about the South Korean star. 

Wiki & Biography

Shin Ryujin was born on 17th April 2000 in the capital city of Seoul South Korea. Presently her age is 19 years old and her zodiac sign is Aries.

The young K-pop star made her way into the entertainment industry at the age of 16. Yes, you heard it right. She was just 16 when she first came to the limelight. 

Interestingly she didn’t begin her career as a singer or rapper in a band. Rather, she was part of a reality TV show and that’s where she got her big break. 

Even though the singer didn’t end up winning the show, she sure caught everyone’s attention and that’s how she bagged her position as lead vocalist, rapper and dancer in JYP Entertainment’s all-girl band.
