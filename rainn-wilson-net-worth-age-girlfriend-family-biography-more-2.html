Publish date: 2024-05-26

Rainn Percival Dietrich Wilson or Rainn Wilson is an American who is a popular actor, comedian, podcaster, producer, and writer by profession. He is in the industry since the year 1993 and is also working for the political party Independent.

He is best known for his portrayal as Dwight Schrute on the NBC comedy The Office, for which he garnered three straight Emmy Award nominations for Outstanding Supporting Actor in a Comedy Series.
Rainn Wilson Wiki/Biography

Born on 20 January 1966, Rainn Wilson’s age is 56 years as of 2022. He was born and raised in a well-settled Baha’i family from Seattle, Washington, the United States. He is an American by nationality and has a belief in the Baha’i religion.
He completed his early schooling at Shorecrest High School.
After that, he enrolled himself at the University of Washington followed by New York University from where he completed his graduation. From the very beginning of his childhood, he was more interested in acting and various co-curricular activities rather than in studying.
