Kelly Ripa shares adorable baby pictures for special occasion

Publish date: 2024-07-09

They couldn't be sweeter

    Kelly Ripa is basking in baby joy! The star looked happy as can be as she posted an adorable baby photo in honor of a special day.

    Fans couldn't help but gush at the pictures and sweet tribute she shared dedicated to her good friend Anderson Cooper's child, Wyatt Morgan Cooper.

    Baby Wyatt was born on 27 April, 2020, and the reporter shares his first son with his ex-partner Benjamin Maisani. The two welcomed their second son just this year, another boy, named Sebastian Luke Maisani-Cooper.
