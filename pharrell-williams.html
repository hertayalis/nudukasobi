Publish date: 2024-08-01

Pharrell Williams has a net worth of $250 million as a recording artist, producer, musician, and fashion designer.

Who is Pharrell Williams?

Pharrell Williams was born in Virginia Beach, Virginia on April 5, 1973. Pharaoh, a handyman, and Carolyn Williams, a teacher, are his parents. Pharrell attended Princess Anne High School, where he marched in the marching band on the snare drum. He went to Northwestern University for two years before dropping out.

Pharrell, the oldest of three kids, began his career in collaboration with boyhood buddy Chad Hugo when they founded The Neptunes to promote and sell the work of pop, hip-hop, and R&B musicians. Hugo and Williams also created the same-named four-piece R&B ensemble. Teddy Riley spotted them as they were performing at a high school talent event. Riley signed the group after they graduated.

How much does Pharrell Williams make annually?

It is estimated that Pharrell Williams earns around $22 million per year through his music and enterprises.

How many businesses does Pharrell Williams own?

Pharrell Williams collaborated on the design of spectacles and jewelry for Louis Vuitton in 2008. Williams worked on a sculpture with Takashi Murakami at Art Basel in 2009. Williams founded I am OTHER, a multimedia creative collaborative and musical label, in May 2012. I am OTHER has its own YouTube channel as well as clothing brands. Billionaire Boys Club and Ice Cream Footwear are two of his clothing lines. Ice Cream Footwear has a store in Tokyo, Japan, while Billionaire Boys Club has a flagship store in New York City.

How many Investments does Pharrell Williams have?

Williams has made massive investments in some brands and items like spectacles and jewelry for Louis Vuitton.

How many Endorsement deals has Pharrell Williams?

Williams has lucrative deals with a couple of brands and companies like Adidas, Beats By Dre, and Billionaire Boys Club.
