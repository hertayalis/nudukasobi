Tracy Tutor- Biography

Tracy Tutor works for Douglas Elliman Beverly Hills, where she consults with some of the world’s most well-known architects and developers and is a key part of the firm’s Sports & Entertainment division. She is also the first and only female cast member of Bravo’s Million Dollar Listing LA.

Tracy Tutor is a successful entrepreneur and television personality who has made a comfortable livelihood. Her net worth is expected to reach $20 million by 2022. In November 2020, she sold her huge Brentwood home for $19.7 million to a Hong Kong billionaire.

Tracy Tutor- Relationship, Married Life

Tracy was married for thirteen years to Jason Maltas, a home contractor, before filing for divorce in February 2018 and having their divorce finalized in January 2019. She had two children with Jason, Juliet, and Scarlett. Tracy and Jason share custody of their two children. Similarly, they agreed to divide their assets equally. When they split, Tracy took custody of their home, which they purchased for $13.5 million in 2009.

Tracy’s current boyfriend is Erik Anderson. Tracy revealed in a People magazine interview in July 2020 that she got the flu, which she suspected was caused by a Coronavirus. At the time, she was in Mexico with Erik. When she returned to the United States, she went to see a doctor discover if she had tested positive for COVID-19. She then discovered that she, too, had been diagnosed with COVID-19.

Tracy Tutor- Professional Career

She started her career in real estate, working for her father at Tutor Perini. During her tenure there, she learned a lot about the real estate business and developed a strong work ethic. Tracy quit the company after three years to pursue a career as a residential real estate agent. She is now a leading real estate agent with Douglas Elliman Beverly Hills, where she specializes in Sports and Entertainment. Tracy has collaborated with well-known real estate businesses as well as architects such as John Lautner, Frank Gehry, and Pierre Koenig. She is also the public face of the West Hollywood Edition Hotel and Residences, which was founded by visionary hotelier Ian Schrager and created by British architect John Pawson.

Tracy had previously worked as a real estate agent with the Agency, Partners Trust, and Sotheby’s International Realty, as well as Douglas Elliman.

The tutor has previously joined the cast of “Million Dollar Listing Los Angeles” for the show’s 10th season in 2017. She was the first female cast member of the show and the series’s second female franchise member. Tracy’s first book, Fear Is Just a Four-Letter Word, will be released on July 14, 2020, by Portfolio, a Penguin Random House division. “The book is an unquestionable must for every competitive woman who wants to win her next business fight before she even enters the room,” according to Penguin Random House.
